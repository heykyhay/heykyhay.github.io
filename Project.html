Welcome,



Welcome, this is designed as a resource to help you during high stress moments in your life. Provided are techniques to help you during anxious moments such as reminding you to go on a walk, breathing techniques and resources on how to locate services in your region.


Full disclosure: We are not doctors and are not giving a medical diagnosis. This app is designed to help you while you wait for mental health services.

Try These 6 Mindful Steps

  1. Cancel Noise
    Listen to your favourite song or podcast.
  2. Drink Water
    or have a cup of calming tea.
  3. Mindful Exercise
    Read a book, go for a short walk, do some Yoga, or Meditate.
  4. Journal
    Create physical distance between our thoughts and physical self.
  5. Get Into Nature
    Try gardening. Remember to touch or hug a tree.
  6. Breathing Exercise
    Scroll down to try our Breathing Exercises.
